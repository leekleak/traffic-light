<p>Traffic Light is an open-source tool to track your network speed and data usage inspired by Internet Speed Meter.</p>
<h2>Why?</h2>
<ul>
<li>Free and open source</li>
<li>Uses few <a href="https://github.com/leekleak/traffic-light/wiki/Permission-Comparison">permissions</a></li>
<li>Uses <a href="https://github.com/leekleak/traffic-light/wiki/Battery-Usage">less battery</a></li>
<li>No tracking</li>
<li>Beautiful UI</li>
<li>Incredibly small app size</li>
</ul>
